Case Study 1

MIS Revenue Report Engine

Built a policy-level revenue reporting engine for insurance MIS using complex Trino SQL. The core challenge was handling different insurers, product categories, retail policies, group policies, and complicated commission rate rules in one reporting logic.

Problem: Revenue calculation rules differed by insurer, product type, policy category, and commission structure.
Solution: Created a 1000+ line Trino SQL report to compute premium, commission, reward, and final revenue at policy level.
Output: Produced MIS-ready revenue data that could be used for reporting, dashboarding, and business review.
Trino SQL 1000+ Lines SQL Retail MIS Group Policies Commission Rates Revenue Reporting
-- Sanitized revenue-reporting pattern
1. Extract retail and group policy records
2. Classify insurer, product, segment and policy type
3. Apply OD, TP, reward and commission rate logic
4. Calculate policy-level premium and revenue
5. Validate totals against MIS reporting checks
6. Prepare final revenue output for dashboards
Case Study 2

3-Year IRDA Data Preparation Pipeline

Prepared IRDA data for three financial years from scratch. The work started from Excel files, moved through Python-based selective column extraction, CSV creation, S3 storage, Trino table creation, and final SQL analysis.

Problem: IRDA data was available in Excel-based formats and needed to be converted into structured analytics-ready tables.
Solution: Used Python to extract required columns, convert Excel into clean CSV files, and store them in an S3 path.
Output: Created Trino tables on top of the processed files and used SQL for year-wise IRDA analysis.
