Case studies

How I approach real analytical problems.

Short, interview-ready explanations focused on the problem, analytical choices and business result.

Insurance Revenue Analytics Engine

Revenue rules varied by insurer, vehicle type, product, fuel, coverage, policy structure and commission component. Manual handling created maintenance and validation risk.

Problem

Create one dependable policy-level output across multiple business rules and source structures.

Approach

Built layered Trino SQL using CTEs, mappings, JSON extraction and explicit validation logic for premium, commission and reward.

Result

Produced a reusable MIS-ready dataset that supports monthly reporting, reconciliation and dashboarding.

3-Year IRDAI Regulatory Data Pipeline

Large Excel files from multiple financial years had inconsistent structures and could not be analysed reliably in their original form.

Problem

Convert changing reporting files into consistent analytical tables without losing auditability.

Approach

Used Python to select and standardise fields, wrote clean CSV outputs to S3, created Trino tables and applied mapping and deduplication logic.

Result

Created a structured multi-year reporting base for analysis, policy mapping and regulatory reporting checks.

Executive MIS & IRDAI Dashboard Suite

Stakeholders needed fast answers to premium, commission, revenue and policy questions without reviewing raw tables.

Problem

Make complex monthly reporting easier to consume and compare across business dimensions.

Approach

Designed KPI cards, trend charts and filters using Google Sheets, Apps Script and a custom HTML/CSS/JavaScript interface.

Result

Enabled stakeholder-ready views across month, segment, merchant, product and performance metrics.

Policy Data Quality & Reconciliation

Policy identifiers and financial fields contained duplicates, nulls, formatting variations and source mismatches.

Problem

Correct records while preserving unaffected fields and prove that the replacement dataset remained consistent.

Approach

Created deterministic matching keys, standardisation rules, latest-record logic and before/after validation queries.

Result

Validated millions of records, isolated unresolved exceptions and achieved zero grouped mismatches in a correction run.

CardPulse Payments Risk Intelligence

A payments business must reduce fraud exposure without creating excessive false positives, review workload or customer friction.

Problem

Prioritise risky transactions and merchants while quantifying the trade-off between recall and operational review volume.

Approach

Generated synthetic relational data, built SQL marts, calculated merchant scores and evaluated multiple risk thresholds.

Result

At a score threshold of 50, the sample queue reviewed about 3.08% of transactions and delivered 3.13× fraud lift.
